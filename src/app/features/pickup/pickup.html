Waste Collection Guidelines

Plastic

Clean bottles, containers, and packaging. Remove caps and labels when possible.

Paper

Newspapers, magazines, cardboard, and office paper. Keep dry and free from food residue.

Glass

Bottles and jars. Rinse clean and remove metal lids.

Organic

Food scraps, yard waste, and compostable materials. Use compostable bags.
